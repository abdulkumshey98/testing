Logo Design

Conceptualizing an effective logo for a company or product can be very challenging and very rewarding. A custom logo design should be able to meet existing design principles and standards like: balance, proximity or unity, alignment, consistency or repetition, white space and contrast. It should also be able to withstand resizing. Whether it’s splashed across a billboard or printed on a business card, it must maintain its integrity and original form – table stakes for any experienced logo design company.
